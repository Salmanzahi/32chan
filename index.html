Our History

Born from the ashes of traditional forums, 32chan emerged in 2023 as a revolutionary platform dedicated to preserving the original spirit of the internet: freedom, anonymity, and unfiltered expression. In an era of increasing digital surveillance and corporate control, we stand as a beacon for those who believe in the fundamental right to privacy online.

Our platform is built with cutting-edge encryption technology and a strict no-logging policy, ensuring that your digital footprint remains yours alone. We're committed to providing a space where ideas can flow freely, without fear of censorship or tracking.
